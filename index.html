About TechFest

TechFest is a collection of events at Monash University Clayton campus to celebrate all things tech - we encourage everyone (not just those in IT courses) to join in and have some fun!

Programs

We have various events going out throughout the week so make sure you sign up to events. Some events are for Wired members only, while others are not, so make sure you join Wired Monash (you can sign up at any of our events).
